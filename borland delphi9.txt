borland delphi 2005 flirt signature file for ida pro by servil
--------------------------------------------------------------

signature was made out of home-compiled runtimes from sourcecode coming along
with the distribution:

  - rtl
  - vcl
  - xml, db, websnap, soap, internet, indy9/10, ibx, intraweb, toolsapi

sigfile recognizes versions from d2005 original to d2005 update3.
full (globalized) names expansion.

install:

copy included delphi9.sig to %ida%\sig\ directory.

enjoy
